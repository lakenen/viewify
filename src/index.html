Use Cases

  • Display your product manuals or restaurant menus on your web site.
  • Display presentations or whitepapers in your blog.
  • Display user-uploaded documents in your web and mobile

Benefits

  • Improved user experience: Instead of downloading PDF, PowerPoint, or other document files, let your users view them instantly without leaving your website.
  • New insights: Gain insight into which documents are being viewed the longest, or even which pages are drawing the most attention.
  • Easy setup: All it takes is one line of JavaScript — just like Google Analytics — and any links on your site pointing to a PDF or Microsoft Office file will automatically use our viewer.

Basics

  • Supports PDFs, Word, and PowerPoint documents
  • Works on web browsers and mobile devices
  • Won’t slow down your site (in fact, documents will display 2X faster on average)
