Documents Reimagined

Viewify converts links to PDF and Office documents to use Box View HTML, CSS, and SVG files that are entirely self-contained and render perfectly on any device. Once converted, Viewify links load instantly, alleviating the burden of downloading large PDF and Office files.

Viewify is as easy as pasting this code into your website:

                            <script>
                            !function(v,i,e,w,d,o,c,s){
                              v[d]={id:o},c=i.createElement(e),s=i.getElementsByTagName(e)[0],
                              c.async=1,c.src=w,s.parentNode.insertBefore(c,s)
                            }(window,document,'script','//s.viewify.me/1/v.js','viewify','V-36433643-1');
                            </script>

This will convert all links to PDFs and Office documents on your site to
automatically use the Box View API to generate beautiful web-friendly documents!
